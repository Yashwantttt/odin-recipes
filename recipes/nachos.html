Nacho-Sauce

Description

This nacho cheese sauce recipe makes a simple sauce that's delicious spread over tortilla chips. Add some sliced jalape√±os to spice things up a bit.

Ingredients

  • Butter and flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Make the roux with butter and flour
  2. Pour in the milk and whisk until thick
  3. Add cheese and salt, then cook until the cheese is melted.
